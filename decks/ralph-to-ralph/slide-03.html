Who

Non-technical founders who want to build or clone a SaaS product.

⚡

The Pain

They know exactly what to build — but can't build and launch it at production quality themselves.

Getting to production takes months or years, requires entire engineering teams, and costs significant money.

REAL SCENARIO

A solo founder sees a product like Resend. They know customers want it. They know exactly which features to copy.

But they get stuck — because turning that into a real app means frontend, backend, infra, APIs, auth, deployment, and ops they cannot do alone.
